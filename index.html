Online Voting

Welcome to our online voting platform! At CR Election Portal , we're passionate about making the democratic process accessible to everyone. Our user-friendly platform prioritizes security and convenience, allowing you to cast your vote from anywhere. Embracing the power of technology, we aim to enhance civic engagement and create a more connected community. Join us in redefining the way we participate in elections, one vote at a time!

Steps For New Users To Vote




> Go to Vote Now


> Enter you Name & full Registration Number


> Fill in the details, and Submit the form


> New page will open, with details of CRs of your respective Class


> Click Vote and Choose the submit option


> There you go! You've succesfully voted for your CR


> For further queries, check out the FAQ section
